The pipeline

Six stages. Run all of them, or run just one.

Every stage picks up the most recently modified file from the one before it, so you can stop, fix something by hand, and pick right back up.

01

Generate Script

Gemini writes narration and a scene breakdown for whatever niche you picked, staying factual to the source and steering clear of subjects you've already covered.

02

Generate Audio

Turns your script into natural narration.

03

Generate Timestamps

Listens back to the narration and marks the exact moment every single word lands.

04

Generate Subtitles

Word-level timing becomes a clean .srt file, attached later as a toggleable caption track instead of burned permanently into the frame.

05

Assemble Video

Drop in your own images and clips. Stills get a Ken Burns pan and zoom, clips play as-is, everything crossfades and lands exactly on the narration's length.

06

Upload to YouTube

Publishes as private with the caption track already attached, so you get the final look before anyone else does.

FAQ

Questions people actually ask

How does the waitlist work?+

Seats open in small batches so the pipeline stays stable while we add niches and iron out bugs. Your position moves up as people ahead of you get in, or faster if people join through your referral link.

Does this cost anything to run?+

The pipeline itself is free. The only thing you supply is your own images or clips.

Do I need to know how to code?+

No. Everything runs through the dashboard: a button per stage, a live console, a script editor, and a pronunciation manager. The command line is there if you want it, not because you need it.

Will this get my channel flagged or banned?+

Every upload lands as private first. You review it, fix anything that's off, and publish it yourself when you're ready. Nothing goes live without you looking at it first.

What if the narrator mispronounces a name?+

Add a pronunciation fix once in the dashboard and it's applied to every script from then on, no re-recording and no digging through files by hand.

Can I use my own footage instead of AI-generated art?+

That's the only way it works. Drop images or clips into the per-video folder, name them so alphabetical order matches playback order, and the assembly stage handles the pans, crossfades, and timing.

What's next for autoslop?+

TikTok upload is next, followed by wrapping the whole pipeline into a scheduled job for genuine daily automation.
